## Select traces on chart

You can press the mouse and drag to select an area of the chart to get the traces in the selected area:

<img src={require('@/img/docs/plugin/select-traces.jpg').default.src} alt="select-traces"  style={{height: '300px'}} className="rounded-lg ring-1 ring-black/5" />

You can also click on a trace point to select that trace.


## Compare traces 

<img src={require('@/img/docs/plugin/compare-traces.jpg').default.src} alt="compare-traces"  style={{height: '200px'}} className="rounded-lg ring-1 ring-black/5" />

Select two traces in the list and click `Compare` button as above, then you can compare the two traces with details:
